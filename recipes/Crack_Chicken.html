Crack Chicken Recipe

I ripped this recipe off of a guy on Youtube. It's a great recipe because it takes less than 15 minutes total, including prep time.

Let's start. Shall we?

Ingredients

  • Chicken (literally any amount)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Any seasoning (try old bay!)
  • Kosher salt

Steps

  1. Lightly season the chicken with kosher salt and other seasonings.
  2. Cover the chicken in mayonnaise. Thin coat is fine, usually no more than a tablespoon per half pound.
  3. Put the chicken into a prewarmed skillet on medium high. Flip the chicken a couple of times to ensure an even golden brown crust. Take the chicken off heat around 155° farenheight and let it sit for 5-10 minutes before cutting.
